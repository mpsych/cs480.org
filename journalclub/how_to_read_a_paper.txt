# How to read a paper

## So many papers..
* you can not read them all

## When to read papers?
* interested in a topic / related to your own work
** you grab an individual paper based on the title and content
* State-of-the-Art Reports (STAR)
** summarize a research field with a lot of references
** include sometimes 100+ papers
* other ways: you skim papers (you need to..)
* peer review: you decide whether a paper is acceptable

## What is a good paper?
* abstract
* figures + captions
* conclusion

## HOW TO READ A PAPER
** Abstract: high-level summary of a paper
